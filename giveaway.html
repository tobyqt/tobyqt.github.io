Toby’s Game Giveaway

I have a ton of un-redeemed Steam codes from all the Humble Bundles I’ve bought over the years. Some are for games I already have on Steam. Some are for games I own on another platform. Others are for games I’m not interested in playing. At least not when my list of “games I want to play” (as well as “books I want to read” and “TV shows I want to watch”) is already so long.

So I’m doing a giveaway. Look through the available games below, think about which ones you want, then go fill this form out. An explanation of how the giveaway works is over there.

There are a lot of popular and well-reviewed games here. I left out a few games that had really bad reviews. I’m not trying to dump trash games onto my friends. But I did list some games with clear flaws (e.g. clunky controls) as long as they still had fans.

For now, I’m limiting the giveaway to people I know. But I’m defining that pretty broadly. If we’ve never really talked, but we’re mutuals on tumblr, or you’re in my Love Nikki association, that still counts as “I know you”. Just tell me how you know me in the giveaway entry form (e.g. “I’m username on tumblr and we’ve followed each other for ages”).
